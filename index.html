About the Company

Manufacturer and Service provider of drones and various Industrial IoT and Automation related products.

  • UAV
  • Automation
  • IIoT

In this world of technology, changes are happening rapidly in the field of artificial intelligence and autonomous machineries, man is being replaced by machineries wherever possible to increase the efficiency of operations and to reduce the life risk. Smart Swift Innovations provides edge to autonomous technology in the field of automation and UAV by manufacturing hybrid drones and devices indigenously by provides services in the various sectors globally. We also provide tailor made solutions to their clients and are known for its versatility in execution of the operations, whether its government or a private platform, we are ready to cater entire industry with our swift services and products.

In the Feild of Education

We believe in educating our generation and to do so a separate wing is managing eduitational affairs under model aviation and advance learning in UAV technology courses.

As Manufacturer

Company manufactures UAV's and IoT related automation products indigenously for commercial enterprise and goverment departments.

As Developer

In addition to the manufacturing there is a research and development wing which looks after the future aspects and situational products.

Drone as a Service

Under current government policies and planning, we are providing mapping drones with DGPS and GIS work to the required sectors in private and government bodies.
